    1. & :link { color: red }
      This will change the <a> tag link color, although if you have visited it once before, it won't take effect since it has already stored in your Cahce Browser, making you have to go either Incognito or clear Cache Memory.

      This will happen if you Hover your Cursor on the link, althouh it won't do anything for now.
